Member Enumeration Documentation

◆ Phase

enum class uni20::async::EpochContext::Phase
strong
Enumerator
Pending 

The epoch is not yet active (i.e. it is in the future)

Started 

The epoch has started but is waiting for a writer task.

Writing 

The epoch is active and waiting for the writers to finish, with no writer currently running.

Reading 

The epoch is active and waiting for readers to finish.

Finished 

The epoch is not active, and readers have finished.
