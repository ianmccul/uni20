Detailed Description

template<class Func, class... Spans>
struct uni20::TransformAccessor< Func, Spans >

An mdspan accessor that applies an N‑ary functor to N child spans, with maximal empty‑base optimizations.

Template Parameters
FuncA callable taking Spans::reference... and returning R.
SpansZero or more mdspan types (must share extents & rank).
