Detailed Description

template<AsyncTaskAwaitable A>
struct uni20::async::AsyncTaskAwaiter< A >

Forwarding awaiter that takes ownership of a std::coroutine_handle and forwards to an awaiter as an AsyncTask.

AsyncTaskAwaiter is a wrapper that manages the transfer of ownership from an AsyncTask into a coroutine_handle and back again.
